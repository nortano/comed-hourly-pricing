Privacy Policy for Hourly Pricing for ComEd

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Hourly Pricing for ComEd" Wear OS application (the "App") handles information when you use the service.

1. Data Collection and Use

The App is designed to provide electricity pricing information with maximum privacy.

  • Personal Information: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • Sensitive Permissions: The App does not request or use sensitive permissions such as Location, Camera, Microphone, or Contacts.
  • Device Information: No unique device identifiers or hardware information are collected by the developer.

2. Third-Party Services

The App fetches electricity pricing data from the public ComEd Hourly Pricing API (https://hourlypricing.comed.com/).

  • When the App requests price updates, ComEd's servers may receive standard technical information typically sent by web clients (such as IP address).
  • We do not provide any of your personal data to ComEd.
  • Please refer to ComEd's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

3. Data Storage and Caching

The App uses Jetpack DataStore to cache the latest electricity prices locally on your Wear OS device. This is used solely to:

  • Provide "Offline Fallback" functionality when a network connection is unavailable.
  • Display pricing information on Watch Face Complications and Tiles efficiently.

This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to any external servers other than the standard network requests to the ComEd API mentioned above.

4. Security

We value your trust in using our App. Since the App does not collect personal data, the risk of data breaches involving your personal information is eliminated.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
